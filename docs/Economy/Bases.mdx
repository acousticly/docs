# Bases

Bases are essential to Acoustic's Economy. It is best you get to know about them.

## Buying a Base

Run the `~base buy` command, and you will get a list of the bases.

> View: ~base buy

> Purchase: ~base buy `ID`

## Base Stats

Luck: The more luck, the better your chances.

Defense: The more defense, the safer you are, and the less likely you will be successfully robbed.

## Upgrades

There are four types of upgrades. (Luck, Defence, Money Printer, Mining)

- The luck and defense upgrades increase those respective stats.

- The money printer upgrades will provide you with money when you send messages, run commands, or both!

- The mining kit will allow you to mine. The more you have, the more beneficial it is to mine.

> ~upgrades buy `ID`
